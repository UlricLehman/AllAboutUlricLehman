I LOVE Orangestar's music! Why do I love Orangestar's music so much? Here are 3 reasons why!

  1. Because it's so uplifting!
    • Even if you don't know the words to the music, the way that the music is written just hits you with a positive energy to where you feel that you feel invincible.
    • Because of this Uplifting mood it's super easy to put it on, and people aren't super against it even if you don't know the words.
  2. Perfect summer music!
    • A lot of the music are talking about summer and how joyful that a time it can be. The freedom that is felt is very amazing.
    • Very often summer music is portrayed in such a negative light, however, this artist truly embraces the freedom that summer can offer, which makes me feel all warm and fuzzy on the inside.
  3. Finally, because the music is challenging to sing.
    • While some people find this to be more of a detractor, I believe that it is a very good thing. Namely it makes it satisfying to be a fan of their music, because it's so fast.
    • It also gives me a good excuse to come back to listen to more.
